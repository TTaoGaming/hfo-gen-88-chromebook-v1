Based on the **Octal Manifest**, the HFO Commander Octet does not fall into a single traditional MTG archetype. Instead, it is a highly evolved **Hybrid Archetype** combining three distinct pillars:

### A. Sliver / Typal Synergy (The Root)

The **HFO type** acts as a global shared-logic substrate. Like Slivers, every Commander ("Sliver") grants its specific function (Sense, Fuse, Shape, etc.) to the rest of the Hive. This creates a "Keyword Soup" environment where the total power of the board is exponential rather than linear.

### B. Artifact Swarm / Go-Wide (The Body)

The reliance on **1/1 HFO Artifact tokens** places it firmly in the "Go-Wide" or "Token Swarm" category. However, unlike standard tokens, these are "Empty Shards" that receive logic from the Commanders, functioning more like a distributed computing cluster.

### C. SOG (Strife-Of-Glory) Aristocrats (The Heart)

The interaction between **The Red Regnant (P4)** and **The Pyre Praetorian (P5)** introduces the **SOG / Aristocrats** engine. You are not just attacking; you are using your own agents as fuel. Killing your own units ("Mutation Killing") triggers **Song of Strife and Glory**, while **Dance of Death and Rebirth** ensures they are never truly gone.
