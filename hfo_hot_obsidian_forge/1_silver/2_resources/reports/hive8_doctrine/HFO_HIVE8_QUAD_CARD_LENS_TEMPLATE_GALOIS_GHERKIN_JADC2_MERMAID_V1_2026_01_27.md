# HFO HIVE8 — Quad Card Lens Template (Galois × Gherkin × JADC2 × Mermaid) (V1)

## Objective

Provide one repeatable “quad lens” scaffold to anchor identity for HFO (and then for each of the 8 Legendary Commanders).

## The Four Lenses

### 1) Galois Lattice Identity (who/where in the octree)

Required fields:

- `port`: P0–P7
- `powerword`: OBSIDIAN letter mapping
- `commander_name`: legendary commander persona
- `anti_diagonal_pair`: port such that $p + q = 7$

### 2) Declarative Gherkin (what “done” means)

Write acceptance criteria as *Given / When / Then*.

Minimum:

- 1 invariant (always true)
- 1 happy path
- 1 fail-closed path

### 3) JADC2 / Mosaic Lens (tactical domain + tile)

Required fields:

- `jadc2_domain`: e.g. ISR / Data Fabric / Delivery / Red Team / Blue Team / AAR / Navigate
- `mosaic_tile_role`: one sentence

### 4) Mermaid Proof (wiring diagram)

Provide one Mermaid diagram that shows:

- the commander
- the token (if any)
- the passive shared effect
- the active shared effect
