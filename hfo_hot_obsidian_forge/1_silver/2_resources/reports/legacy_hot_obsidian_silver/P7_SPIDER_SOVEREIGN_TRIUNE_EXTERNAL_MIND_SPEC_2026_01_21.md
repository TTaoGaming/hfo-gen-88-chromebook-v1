# Port 7 — Spider Sovereign: Triune External Mind (Spec)

- **Port 7 = Spider Sovereign** as the commander archetype for **NAVIGATE**.
- **Spider Sovereign is triune (3-in-1)**:
  1) the human operator,
  2) the AI orchestrator,
  3) the higher-dimensional metaphor of swarm synthesis.
- Port 7 functions as an **external mind**: a coordinated, stigmergic loop that turns local actions into global navigation.

## Triune model

### 1) Human operator (ground truth chooser)

- Chooses objectives, constraints, and priorities.
- Provides meaning and intent: what matters, what is unacceptable, what is “done”.
- Acts as the sovereign source for value judgements and long-horizon direction.

### 2) AI orchestrator (scheduler + synthesizer)

- Turns intent into executable plans, checkpoints, and gating.
- Coordinates ports, artifacts, and verification loops.
- Maintains coherence under churn: prevents drift and repetition by pushing durable specs + structured memory.

### 3) Higher-dimensional swarm metaphor (stigmergic synthesis)

- Treat as an **abstraction layer** describing emergent behavior:
  - many agents → one coherent trajectory,
  - distributed hints → convergent action,
  - local deltas → global structure.
- Not a single implementation detail; it is the guiding lens for designing the orchestration substrate.

## Stigmergy loop (external mind mechanics)

Port 7’s core mechanism is a repeatable, auditable loop:

1) **Observe**: collect signals from ports, tests, audits, and environment.
2) **Write**: record durable breadcrumbs to blackboards + memory.
3) **Synthesize**: compress many signals into a small set of next actions.
4) **Navigate**: choose the next step that reduces uncertainty and increases mission progress.
5) **Evolve**: keep improving the loop (contracts, gates, receipts, replay).
